RaSaIn Education is the largest technology company in Indonesia that focuses on education-based services.
We already have more than 15,000,000 users and manage 100,000 teachers who offer services in more than 80 subject areas.

RaSaIn Education develops a variety of technology-based learning services, including virtual class services,
online exam platforms, subscription learning videos, private les marketplace, as well as other
educational content that can be accessed through the RaSaIn Education web.

The company was founded in 2019 by three friends namely Dinda Salma, Indira Mai and Rafel Andhika,
all of whom made it into the ranks of successful entrepreneurs.
